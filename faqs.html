How to find word count using word counter tool?

Simply type your text into the blank field; the total number of words will increase as you type. Otherwise, simply copy the text and paste it into the empty space; the total number of words will be displayed.

How to find the Character Count using the Character Count tool?

Type your text into the space, and the character count will be updated as you type. Alternatively, copy and paste your text into the space, and your character count will be provided.

What is the word count for 1 page?

A good estimate of the word count for font size 12 pt and single spaced in 1 page is 500 words.

How you should increase your word count?

Add examples in your points or explain your points more elaborately are quick ways to increase your word count.

How you should reduce your word count?

Use active voice instead of passive voice in your sentences, eliminate unnecessary words are quick ways to reduce your word count.

How do you count words in essay?

Simply copy and paste your essay into our word counter tool, and you will get the word count for your essay.
